Professional FinFlow App Icon Design

Design Concept:
- Modern, professional finance app icon
- Clean blue gradient background (#0A2540 to #1a3a5a)
- White circular border with subtle glow
- Animated finance chart with upward trend
- Currency symbols ($ and ₹) to represent global finance
- Smooth animations for dynamic feel

Key Elements:
1. Background: Deep blue gradient with circular shape
2. Finance Chart: Three stacked bars with upward trend arrow
3. Animation: Pulsing effect on bars and fade animation on arrow
4. Currency Elements: Subtle $ and ₹ symbols
5. Shine Effect: Subtle highlight for premium feel
6. Border Glow: Soft blue glow around the icon

This design represents:
- Financial growth and stability
- Modern technology and innovation
- Global accessibility
- Professional reliability

The icon should be exported in multiple sizes for Android and iOS platforms.
